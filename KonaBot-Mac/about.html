About KonaBot

KonaBot is a Mac app that download wallpapers from konachan.com regularly and set them as your desktop wallpaper.

This app uses the following third party libraries:

  • AFNetworking
  • ReactiveObjC

For bug reports, please go to GitHub repo and open an issue, or email me at email@hkalexling.com
